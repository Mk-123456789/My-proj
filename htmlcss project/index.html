Internship Opportunities

Our commitment to sustainable growth in partnership with our communities drives our
investment in the local talent pipeline through internship programmes. These
programmes offer real life business and professional experience which provide a
foundation for fruitful future careers.

We currently offer internship opportunities only in Nigeria in line with applicable local
laws.

Graduate Opportunities

Our flagship Young Professionals Development (YPD) Programme provides an
exciting career start and a clear path to professional and business leadership for
graduates. The 3-year programme combines foundational technical and business
training, early career coaching and mentoring, immersive competency-based
development opportunities across various roles to prepare the right quality of future
leaders for our business.
